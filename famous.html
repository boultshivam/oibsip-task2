Personal Life

Greats are not born every day; they are born once in a century and are remembered for
millennials to come. One such great, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, was born in Rameswaram of Madras
Presidency on 15th October 1931 to a poor Tamil Muslim family. He lived with his family in
the temple city of Tamilnadu, Rameswaram, where his father, Jainulabdeen, had a boat and was
an imam of a local mosque. At the same time, his mother, Ashiamma, was a housewife. Kalam had
four brothers and one sister in his family, from which he was the youngest.
